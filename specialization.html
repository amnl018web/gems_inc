What We Do

Those important moments in your life are special. They should be celebrated and honored,
but planning for events can be hard. That's why we're here. We specialize in planning
for Milestone Celebrations, Weddings and Creative Remembrances.

We craft creative concepts and designs, facilitate effective negotiations and coordination with vendors and suppliers, and ensure seamless execution to make each event become an extraordinary and memorable experience.

Milestone Celebrations

The milestones in our life mark an important moment in our growth and development as individuals. We help our clients commemorate these momentous events by crafting unique celebrations that reflect their personality and vision. Our goal is to help make our clients' milestone events be what they're meant to be - memorable celebrations.

  • Birthday Parties
  • Debut
  • Baptism
  • Graduation Parties
  • Etc.

Weddings

We help soon-to-wed couples plan unique weddings and wedding-related ceremonies. We recognize that every couple is unique. We tailor our services to suit their needs and bring their vision of their perfect wedding to life. We aim to make the start of their happily ever after a joyous and extraordinary occasion.

  • Wedding Ceremony
  • Wedding Reception
  • Despedida de Sol Tera
  • Bridal Shower
  • Bachelor's Party
  • Etc.

Creative Remembrances

We help create personalized end-of-life ceremonies that reflect and honor the vibrant life of the dearly departed. Planning for such ceremonies can be daunting and overwhelming for the bereaved. Our goal is to help them alleviate some of that burden by working hand-in-hand with them in order to create a ceremony that reflect, celebrate and pay tribute to the incredible life lived by their departed loved one.
